1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
BYTE SYZE LAHRI is a micro‑food operation optimized for speed, portability, and single‑item specialization. Designed for youth sports venues, school events, and community gatherings, the LAHRI cart delivers a $10 entry price point with a simple, high‑margin 3‑item output menu. Core asset: a modified golf‑cart food truck with fast reset cycles and minimal kitchen requirements.
2. PRODUCT MODEL
IN: $10 OUT: - Pizza Slice (pre-cooked, vendor-loaded) - Soft Drink (fountain) - Cookie (warm) - Cookie + Ice Cream (premium) "Single Food Specializes" categories: - Bowls - Sandwiches - Locho - Noodles - Pizza Slice - Treats Each category is modular and can be swapped depending on venue demand.

1 cart → $108k–$144k/yr 5 carts → $540k–$720k/yr 10 carts → $1.08M–$1.44M/yr Scaling advantages: - Bulk ingredient purchasing - Shared maintenance crew - Centralized charging + cleaning - Multi-venue rotation schedule
8. OPERATIONS MODEL
KITCHEN: - Minimal prep - Pre-cooked pizza loaded from vendor - Warmers + fountain + cookie heater - 10-minute reset cycle CART: - Golf-cart chassis - Battery-powered warmers - Modular food bay - POS terminal or automated vending VENUE: - Youth sports fields - School events - Community centers - Parking lots - Seasonal fairs
9. RISK + MITIGATION
Low foot traffic ............... rotate venues Ingredient spoilage ............ vendor-loaded slices Labor volatility ............... automation Weather ......................... indoor partnerships Cart downtime ................... modular parts
10. PHILOSOPHY
Small menu. Fast service. High margin. One cart = one specialty. Reset fast. Move fast. Serve fast.
